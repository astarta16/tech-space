Who We Are

Tech Space Academy is a premier programming academy dedicated to teaching school students the art of coding. Founded by Nini Gedeshuri, our mission is to empower the next generation of developers and innovators through high-quality education and hands-on learning experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and educate young minds to become proficient in programming and technology. We believe in providing a supportive and engaging learning environment where students can explore their interests, develop their skills, and build a strong foundation for their future careers.
