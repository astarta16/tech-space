About Us

Learn more about Tech Space Academy

Who We Are

Tech Space Academy is a premier programming academy dedicated to teaching school students the art of coding. Founded by Nini Gedeshuri, our mission is to empower the next generation of developers and innovators through high-quality education and hands-on learning experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and educate young minds to become proficient in programming and technology. We believe in providing a supportive and engaging learning environment where students can explore their interests, develop their skills, and build a strong foundation for their future careers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer? +

We offer a variety of programming courses, including Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and more. Our courses are designed to cater to different skill levels, from beginners to advanced students.

What age group is Tech Space Academy for? +

Tech Space Academy is designed for school students, typically aged 8-18. We tailor our programs to suit different age groups and learning abilities.

Do you offer any certifications? +

Yes, we provide certificates upon the successful completion of our courses. These certificates can be a valuable addition to your academic portfolio.

Are your courses online or in-person? +

We offer both online and in-person courses. Our online courses provide flexibility, while our in-person courses offer hands-on learning experiences.

How can I enroll in a course? +

You can enroll in our courses through our website. Simply select the course you are interested in and follow the enrollment process.

What is the cost of your courses? +

The cost of our courses varies depending on the type and duration of the course. Please visit our Courses page for detailed pricing information.
